HISTORY

Real Madrid was founded in 1902 and quickly became one of the most successful football clubs in the world. Based in Madrid, Spain, the club built a legendary reputation through dominance in Spanish and European competitions. Known for its iconic white kit and winning mentality, Real Madrid has produced many legendary players and unforgettable moments. With numerous league titles and European trophies, the club represents excellence, tradition, and the relentless pursuit of greatness in football history. ⚽👑

SANTIAGO BERNABEU STADIUM

The Santiago Bernabéu Stadium is the home of Real Madrid and one of the most famous football stadiums in the world. Located in Madrid, Spain, it opened in 1947 and was named after the legendary club president Santiago Bernabéu.The stadium has hosted countless historic matches, European finals, and unforgettable football moments. After a major modern renovation completed in the 2020s, the Bernabéu now features a futuristic design, a retractable roof, and advanced technology, making it one of the most modern stadiums in world football.

LEGENDS

Four legendary players have shaped the greatness of Real Madrid. Alfredo Di Stéfano led the club’s dominance in the 1950s and helped win multiple European Cups. Cristiano Ronaldo became the club’s all-time top scorer and delivered many historic victories. Raúl González symbolized loyalty and leadership for over a decade. Zinedine Zidane amazed fans with his elegance and unforgettable goals, becoming a key figure in Real Madrid’s rich football legacy.

TROPHIES

Real Madrid is one of the most decorated football clubs in history with an incredible trophy collection. The club has won 36 La Liga titles, 20 Copa del Reys, 15 UEFA Champions League trophies, 13 Spanish Super Cups, 6 UEFA Super Cups, and 8 FIFA Club World/Intercontinental Cups, plus other honours in Europe and worldwide. Altogether, Real Madrid’s total trophy count exceeds 131 and more major titles, showcasing unmatched success and legendary dominance in football.

GAMES

Matches played by Real Madrid are known for excitement, passion, and unforgettable moments.
