ABOUT US

Hi, we are Real Madrid, one of the most popular football teams in Spain. With a legacy built on passion, excellence, and countless victories, we represent more than just a club—we embody the spirit of competition and unity that inspires millions of fans worldwide. Our commitment to greatness on and off the pitch continues to shape our story, making Real Madrid a symbol of pride, tradition, and ambition.

Founded in 1902, Real Madrid has become a symbol of football excellence, with a history filled with legendary players and unforgettable victories. Guided by values of passion, respect, and ambition, we continue to inspire millions of fans across the globe. More than a club, we are a family united by the love of the game.
